Project Purpose and Goal

There wasn't a brief from the client regarding the design, layout or anything about the site. They said they only needed an online presence to increase their appeal as a startup and attract more customers.

Problems and Thought Process

For this project, I didn't have much in the way of problems. All I needed to do was find the right design to suit the client's needs. Secondly, I got free images to use to portray the services offered as the company was new and didn't have any of its own.
